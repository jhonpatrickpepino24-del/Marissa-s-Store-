HISTORY OF THE BUSINESS

Marissa’s Sari-Sari Store in Baclayan, Boljoon, Cebu was established by Marissa Viñan as a small family-run business.

It was created to support daily household needs and provide convenient access to basic goods for the community.

The store started small, selling snacks, beverages, canned goods, and toiletries.

Through hard work and dedication, it became a trusted store in the neighborhood.
FEATURE THE SUCCESS STORY OF THE BUSINESS

Marissa’s Store is a successful sari-sari store built through perseverance and honesty.

Starting with small capital, Marissa focused on providing daily essentials.

Good customer service and proper management helped the store grow.

Despite challenges, the business continues to support her family and community.
FEATURE THE CHALLENGES MET BY THE OWNER

Marissa Viñan faced limited capital, competition, and supply problems.

Rising prices and customer utang affected income.

Long working hours and weather conditions made the work difficult.
HOW WILL YOU SEE YOUR BUSINESS 10 YEARS AFTER?

I see my business becoming successful, stable, and continuously growing.
HOW DID THE PHILIPPINE GOVERNMENT HELP THE BUSINESS GROW?

The Philippine government helps small store owners through financial assistance and support programs.

This assistance helps small businesses continue and grow.
